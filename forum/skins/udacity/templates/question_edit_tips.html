Be Descriptive

Providing a rich description will increase the likelihood that your fellow students and Udacity staff will understand your question and provide you with an accurate answer.

Add Value

Making sure your posts haven’t already been addressed, before posting, will reduce redundant content in the discussion forum. This will ultimately make the discussion forum easier to search and browse.

Be Respectful

Remember that Udacity students bring to the table diverse perspectives and experience levels with course content. In order for the discussion forum to thrive, it’s important that all are comfortable posting to it. For obvious reasons, this won’t happen in the face of disrespect.
